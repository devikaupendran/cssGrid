Nature

Nature, in the broadest sense is the natural , physical or material world or universe. "Nature" can refer to the phenomena of the physical world and also to the life in general

The word nature is derived from the latin word natura or 'essential qualities innate disposition", and in ancient philosophy, Natura is most used as the latin translation of the Greek word physics ,which is originallt related to the intrinsic characteristics that plants, animals and other features
